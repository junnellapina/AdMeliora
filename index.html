Our Mission

The mission of our channel transcends mere entertainment; it is dedicated to cultivating a vibrant, secure, and all-encompassing online haven. Within this space, high-school students are not only entertained but also encouraged to actively participate, fostering a community that is both inclusive and engaging. Through our dynamic and interactive Q & A live streams, we aim to provide a platform where students can freely ask questions, share their diverse experiences, and forge connections that transcend traditional boundaries. Our ultimate goal is to facilitate a collaborative learning environment, empowering high-school students to glean insights from one another and collectively navigate the rich tapestry of shared knowledge and wisdom.

Our Vision

Our vision is to evolve into the quintessential go-to source for high school students in their quest for knowledge, entertainment, sagacious advice, and unwavering support amidst the diverse tapestry of challenges and triumphs woven into the fabric of their high school experience. We aim to be not just a resource, but a reliable companion, fostering a community where students can find comprehensive answers, engaging entertainment, empathetic advice, and a strong support system to navigate the intricacies of their educational journey with resilience and success.

Stream Outline

Having an outline is a vital step in preparing for a live-stream as it gives it a sense of organization. It lets the streamers efficiently organize and divide their stream into varying parts, making the flow of the stream orderly. The importance of an outline is crucial to the audience as well since it lets them have an overview of what our stream will cover, which may be a determining factor in whether or not they will tune in. As such, below we have prepared a stream outline for our first live stream.
